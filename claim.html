How claiming works

We protect owners and finders. Claims require basic answers about the item and one or more proof files. Moderators approve matches and connect you securely.

  • Describe unique identifiers (stickers, case color, engravings)
  • Upload a past photo or purchase receipt if available
  • Your contact stays private until a match is confirmed

Tip: Accurate descriptions speed up verification.

Photos of you with the item, serial numbers, bills/receipts, or any unique identifiers are best.

Most claims are reviewed within 24–72 hours depending on the queue and complexity.
